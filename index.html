Privacy Policy

Effective January 2026 · Crowdfunding & General Use

QUNIX ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors and backers. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a contribution through our crowdfunding programme.

Information We Collect

When you make a contribution or submit an enquiry, we may collect: your full name, email address, postal address (where required for physical rewards), and payment information. Payment data is processed exclusively through our third-party payment processor and is never stored on QUNIX servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your contribution and issue confirmation receipts.
  • To communicate updates on the campaign, project milestones, and reward fulfilment.
  • To deliver physical or digital rewards to eligible backers.
  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • To comply with applicable legal and financial record-keeping obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared only with: our payment processor (for transaction fulfilment), logistics partners (for physical reward delivery), and regulatory authorities where legally required.

Data Retention

Backer data is retained for a minimum of seven years to comply with financial and tax reporting obligations. You may request deletion of non-statutory data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We may use essential session cookies solely for website functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our operations evolve. Material changes will be communicated by email to registered backers. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance.

Refund & Return Policy

Crowdfunding Contributions · QUNIX · January 2026

QUNIX is grateful for the support of every backer. This policy outlines the circumstances under which contributions may be refunded and the process for requesting a refund.

Contributions Are Generally Non-Refundable

Because crowdfunding contributions are committed to ongoing development costs from the time of receipt, all contributions are considered final and non-refundable except in the specific circumstances outlined below.

Refund Eligibility

  • Campaign Cancellation: If QUNIX officially cancels the crowdfunding campaign before its stated closing date, all contributions received will be refunded in full.
  • Duplicate Transactions: If a technical error results in a duplicate charge, the duplicate amount will be refunded upon verification.
  • Fraudulent Transactions: If a contribution is made fraudulently without the account holder's authorisation, a full refund will be issued upon investigation.
  • Unreceived Physical Rewards: If a physical reward has not been received within 90 days of the stated dispatch date, backers may request a replacement or partial refund of the reward component of their contribution.

Excluded Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued on the basis of: project timeline delays, changes to vehicle specifications, failure to achieve specific technical milestones, or personal change of mind after contribution.

How to Request a Refund

Email [email protected] with your full name, the email address used at contribution, your contribution amount, and the reason for your request. We will acknowledge within 5 business days and resolve eligible claims within 30 days.

Processing

Approved refunds will be returned to the original payment method. Processing times are typically 5–10 business days after approval.

Our Launch Vehicle Programme

Prototype Demonstrator · SLV

Speck

Semi-Reusable Launch Vehicle Demonstrator

Speck is a small-scale reusable launch vehicle demonstrator developed to validate core technologies required for propulsive vertical landing and rapid vehicle reuse. The programme focuses on iterative flight testing of guidance, navigation, and control systems; propulsion performance; and structural reusability under repeated flight loads.

About Us

Next-Generation Aerospace

Who We Are

We are a next-generation aerospace company pushing the boundaries of reusable launch technology. Founded by a team of engineers and visionaries, QUNIX is committed to making space more accessible, affordable, and sustainable for everyone on Earth.

Our Mission

To democratize access to space through innovative, low-cost, reusable launch systems. We believe the next era of space exploration demands vehicles that can fly again and again — without sacrificing reliability or performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an investment?

No. Contributions are voluntary support and do not confer equity, shares, or financial return. See our Terms & Conditions for full details.

Can I get a refund?

Contributions are generally non-refundable as funds are committed to active development. Refunds are available in limited circumstances — see our Refund Policy.

When do I get my digital rewards?

For Razorpay payments, rewards are emailed within 48 hours of payment confirmation. For UPI, email your UTR to [email protected] to trigger fulfilment.

What is the joiner letter?

An official, personally addressed digital letter from QUNIX welcoming you as a mission backer — suitable for saving or printing.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. Razorpay is PCI-DSS compliant. QUNIX never stores your card or UPI details.

What currency can I pay in?

Razorpay accepts INR. For international contributions, please use the contact page and we will arrange an alternative.
