About Us

Next-Generation Aerospace

Who We Are

We are a next-generation aerospace company pushing the boundaries of reusable launch technology. Founded by a team of engineers and visionaries, velocity is committed to making space more accessible, affordable, and sustainable for everyone on Earth.

Our Mission

To democratize access to space through innovative, low-cost, reusable launch systems. We believe the next era of space exploration demands vehicles that can fly again and again — without sacrificing reliability or performance.
